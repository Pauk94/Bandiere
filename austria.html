AUSTRIA

Austria is a landlocked country in Central Europe, known for its rich history, stunning Alpine landscapes, and classical music heritage. Its capital is Vienna, a city renowned for its imperial palaces, museums, and music scene, particularly related to famous composers like Mozart, Beethoven, and Strauss. 🇦🇹 Austria is known for: 🏰 History & Culture – The Habsburg dynasty, Schönbrunn Palace, and the iconic Vienna State Opera 🎶 Music & Arts – Classical music, Viennese waltz, and famous composers like Mozart and Beethoven ⛰ Nature & Landscapes – The Austrian Alps, pristine lakes, and hiking trails like in Tyrol and Salzkammergut 🍰 Food – Wiener Schnitzel (breaded veal), Sachertorte (chocolate cake), and Apfelstrudel (apple pastry) 🏒 Sports – Skiing and snowboarding in the Alps, as well as ice hockey and football
