Bangladesh

Bangladesh is a vibrant country in South Asia, located on the Bay of Bengal and bordered by India and Myanmar. Its capital is Dhaka, one of the largest and busiest cities in the world. Bangladesh is known for its rich culture, history, and natural beauty, as well as its economic growth in recent years. 🇧🇩 Bangladesh is known for: 🏞 Natural Beauty – The Sundarbans mangrove forest (home to the Bengal tiger), Cox’s Bazar (the world's longest natural sea beach), and the tea gardens of Sylhet 🏛 History & Heritage – The Bengal region’s ancient history, the Liberation War of 1971, and historical sites like the Lalbagh Fort in Dhaka 🍛 Food – Hilsa fish, Panta Bhat (fermented rice), Bhuna Khichuri, and Sweets like Roshogolla and Mishti Doi (sweetened yogurt) 🎶 Music & Arts – Baul folk music, Rabindra Sangeet (songs by Rabindranath Tagore), and colorful festivals like Pohela Boishakh (Bengali New Year) 🏏 Sports – Cricket is by far the most popular sport, with the national team enjoying a growing fanbase
