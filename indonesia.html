INDONESIA

Indonesia is a vast and diverse country in Southeast Asia, made up of over 17,000 islands, including Java, Sumatra, Bali, and Borneo. Its capital is Jakarta, located on the island of Java. Indonesia is known for its rich culture, stunning landscapes, and incredible biodiversity. 🌏 Indonesia is famous for: 🏝 Beautiful Islands & Nature – Bali, Komodo Island, Raja Ampat, Mount Bromo 🕌 Culture & Heritage – Borobudur & Prambanan temples, Batik textiles, and traditional dances 🍛 Delicious Food – Nasi Goreng (fried rice), Rendang (spicy beef), Satay (grilled skewers), and Gado-Gado (vegetable salad) 🐉 Wildlife – Orangutans in Borneo, Komodo dragons, and diverse marine life ⚽ Sports – Badminton is very popular, along with football
