JAPAN

Japan is an island nation in East Asia, known for its rich culture, advanced technology, and stunning natural landscapes. It consists of four main islands—Honshu, Hokkaido, Kyushu, and Shikoku—along with many smaller ones. The capital is Tokyo, one of the world's most vibrant and bustling cities. Japan is famous for: 🇯🇵 Culture & Traditions – Samurai, Geisha, Tea Ceremony, and Shinto Shrines 🍣 Food – Sushi, Ramen, Tempura, and Wagyu Beef 🗻 Landmarks – Mount Fuji, Kyoto Temples, Hiroshima Peace Memorial 🎌 Pop Culture – Anime, Manga, J-Pop, and Video Games
