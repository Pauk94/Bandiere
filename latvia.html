LATVIA

Latvia is a beautiful country in the Baltic region of Northern Europe. Its capital is Riga, a city known for its stunning Art Nouveau architecture and vibrant culture. Latvia has a rich history, breathtaking nature, and deep-rooted traditions. 🇱🇻 Latvia is known for: 🏰 Culture & History – Medieval castles, folk songs (dainas), and the Baltic Way protest 🌲 Nature – Dense forests, sandy beaches along the Baltic Sea, and the Gauja National Park 🍲 Food – Rye bread, grey peas with bacon, smoked fish, and Piragi (bacon-filled pastries) 🎶 Festivals – The Latvian Song and Dance Festival, Jāņi (Midsummer celebration) ⚽ Sports – Ice hockey is the most popular sport
