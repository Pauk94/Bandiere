Monaco

Monaco is a tiny but glamorous country on the French Riviera, known for its wealth, luxury, and stunning Mediterranean coastline. It is the second smallest country in the world (after Vatican City) and is ruled by the Grimaldi royal family. The capital is Monte Carlo, famous for its casinos and high-end lifestyle. 🇲🇨 Monaco is known for: 🏎 Formula 1 – The Monaco Grand Prix, one of the most prestigious races in the world 🎰 Luxury & Casinos – The famous Casino de Monte-Carlo and exclusive hotels 🚤 Yachts & Port – Port Hercules is filled with luxury yachts 🏰 Royal Family – Prince Albert II and the glamorous legacy of Princess Grace Kelly 🌅 Scenic Beauty – The Mediterranean coastline, Exotic Garden, and Larvotto Beach ⚽ Sports – AS Monaco FC, a top football club competing in France’s Ligue 1
