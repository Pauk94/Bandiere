NIGERIA

Nigeria is a vibrant and diverse country in West Africa, known for its rich culture, booming economy, and incredible natural beauty. It is the most populous country in Africa, with over 200 million people, and its capital is Abuja, while the largest city is Lagos—a major economic hub. 🇳🇬 Nigeria is known for: 🎭 Culture & Diversity – Over 250 ethnic groups, with the major ones being Hausa, Yoruba, and Igbo 🎶 Music & Entertainment – Afrobeats (Burna Boy, Wizkid, Davido), Nollywood (one of the world's largest film industries) 🍲 Food – Jollof rice, Pounded yam with Egusi soup, Suya (spicy grilled meat), Akara (bean cakes) 🌍 Landmarks & Nature – Zuma Rock, Olumo Rock, Yankari National Park, Idanre Hills ⚽ Sports – Football is the most popular sport, with stars like Jay-Jay Okocha and Victor Osimhen
