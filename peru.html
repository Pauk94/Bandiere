Peru

Peru is a beautiful and diverse country in South America, known for its rich history, breathtaking landscapes, and vibrant culture. The capital is Lima, a coastal city famous for its colonial architecture and world-class cuisine. 🇵🇪 Peru is known for: 🏛 History & Ancient Civilizations – The Inca Empire, Machu Picchu, and Nazca Lines 🌿 Nature & Geography – The Amazon Rainforest, Andes Mountains, and Lake Titicaca 🍲 Food – Ceviche (marinated seafood), Lomo Saltado (stir-fried beef), and Aji de Gallina (spicy chicken stew) 🎭 Culture & Festivals – Inti Raymi (Festival of the Sun), traditional Quechua and Aymara heritage ⚽ Sports – Football is the most popular sport, and the national team has passionate supporters
