Ucraina

Ukraine is a country in Eastern Europe, known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and beautiful landscapes. The capital is Kyiv, one of the oldest cities in Europe, famous for landmarks like St. Sophia's Cathedral and Independence Square. Ukraine is known for: 🇺🇦 Culture & History – Cossack heritage, traditional embroidery (vyshyvanka), and deep folk traditions 🍲 Food – Borscht (beet soup), Varenyky (dumplings), Holubtsi (stuffed cabbage rolls) 🏰 Landmarks – Carpathian Mountains, Lviv’s old town, Chernobyl Exclusion Zone ⚽ Sports – Passion for football, with clubs like Dynamo Kyiv and Shakhtar Donetsk
